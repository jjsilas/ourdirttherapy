Log: Marino 26" Frame Order

Phase: Manufacturing (Peru) • Expected Arrival: May 2026

The Design Intent

For this build, the goal was a 26" Street Trials geometry using Heat Treated CrMo 4130 tubing. The focus is on technical precision and durability for "The Science of Thrill."

Key Geometry Data

  • Reach: 400mm
  • Chainstay: 380mm
  • BB Drop: +10mm
  • Head Tube Angle: 71°
  • Finish: Raw with Matte Black graphics

This frame is designed around a 478mm axle-to-crown fork, specifically for the Manitou Circus 100mm setup.
