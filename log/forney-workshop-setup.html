Forney Workshop Relocation

Project Phase: Site Evaluation & Requirements Mapping

The Objective

Transitioning from a limited backyard setup to a dedicated technical workspace in North Texas. The goal is to establish a formal environment for motorcycle maintenance, video production, and trials practice.

Technical Requirements

  • Space: 3-Car Garage minimum for specialized gear storage.
  • Location: Forney, Nevada, or Fate area.
  • Infrastructure: Dedicated power for the M1 Mac mini/M2 MacBook Air editing suite.
  • Outdoor: Sufficient acreage for a formal trials skills course.

Current Practice Ground

Current informal pallet setup used for Beta 300RR skills drills. This will be superseded by the Forney layout.
